How the work process works

Whether it is a cybersecurity audit, a front-end website build or a safe website add-on, every project starts with clear scope and ends with a defined handover.

Cybersecurity audit process

One-off testing with written permission and a final report. No ongoing monitoring or emergency support included.

1

Scope

We agree what can be tested, what must not be touched, and the safest testing window.

2

Permission

Testing only begins after written authorisation from the business owner or responsible person.

3

Audit

Security tools and manual checks are used responsibly to identify realistic risks.

4

Report

You receive findings, risk levels, screenshots, explanations and practical recommendations.

Website creation and add-on process

Front-end websites only, with optional safe integrations such as forms, booking embeds, newsletter signup, analytics and payment links.

1

Brief

We agree the pages, content, style, add-ons and business goals before design begins.

2

Design

The website layout is created for desktop and mobile using a clean business-focused structure.

3

Build

The front-end pages and agreed third-party integrations are built and prepared for hosting or handover.

4

Handover

You receive the finished site files or upload support, plus a simple handover note.

Clear boundaries protect both sides.

Services are fixed-scope and project-based. Website creation can include safe third-party integrations, but does not include custom backend development, ecommerce systems or account-based portals. Security audits are a snapshot of the agreed scope at the time of testing.

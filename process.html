How the audit process works

Every audit starts with written permission and a clear scope. No aggressive testing, no surprises, no cowboy behaviour.

1

Scope

We agree what can be tested, what must not be touched, and the safest testing window.

2

Permission

Testing only begins after written authorization from the business owner or responsible person.

3

Audit

Security tools and manual checks are used responsibly to identify realistic risks.

4

Report

You receive findings, risk levels, screenshots, explanations and practical recommendations.

Not a corporate pentest. A practical security health check.

This service is designed for small businesses that need to understand obvious risks before they become expensive problems. For highly regulated environments or full CREST penetration testing, a specialist certified consultancy may be recommended.
